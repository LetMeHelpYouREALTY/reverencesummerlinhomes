## 2. COMMUNITY OVERVIEW

### Development History

- **Developer:** Pulte Homes (a publicly traded national homebuilder)
- **Construction period:** 2003–2008 (some sources note 2003–2009)
- **Total homes:** 1,526 single-family homes (per FirstService Residential press release)
- **Sub-neighborhoods:** 9 distinct villages/parcels with varying security tiers

### Security & Access

- **Guard-gated:** The Palms sub-neighborhood has 24/7 staffed guard gate
- **Gated (card/fob):** Silverlake, Windermere (gated entry without staffing)
- **Non-gated:** Amberly, Clairbrook, Eastpoint, Greenfield, Parkfield, Somerset
- Guard house on Silverstone Ranch Drive with landscaped arrival experience
- Active neighborhood watch program

### HOA Structure

- **Master Association:** Silverstone Ranch Community Association
- **Current Management:** CAMCO (as of 2021+; previously FirstService Residential took over January 1, 2021)
- **Sub-associations:** The Palms, Tuscany & The Cottages, Pinehurst (each layer additional services)
- **Master HOA Dues:** $200–$286/month depending on sub-association
- **What dues cover:** 24/7 guard staffing (The Palms), landscaping of common areas, amenity maintenance, community events, reserve funding
- **Rental policy:** Leases must be 6+ months; tenants must register with guard gate; properties must maintain landscape standards
- **CC&Rs:** Deed restrictions enforce community standards; golf course requires 75% homeowner vote to change use

### Community Amenities

- 6-acre central family park (main community hub)
- 3 putting greens
- Lighted tennis courts
- Basketball courts
- Pickleball courts
- Playgrounds / tot lots
- Picnic and BBQ areas
- Walking and biking trails (traces former golf course fairways)
- Lush greenbelts and landscaped parkways
- Seasonal community events: outdoor movie nights, fitness classes, themed tastings, youth camps
- Clubhouse meeting rooms and covered patios available for private reservation
- HOA portal for community communications and document access

## 3. HOME TYPES & ARCHITECTURAL CHARACTER

### Sub-Neighborhood Home Profiles

**The Palms (Guard-Gated Core)**

- Style: Estate single-family, 1–2 story
- Size: 1,900–4,050+ sq ft (description page lists 2,800–4,000+ sq ft for larger estate homes)
- Bedrooms: 3–5
- Garages: 2–3 car
- Lot sizes: Up to 1/3 acre
- Built: 2004–2009
- Features: Casitas, multi-generational suites, courtyard fireplaces, private driveways, golf/mountain views on select lots, EV-ready outlets
- Price range today: $700K–$1.1M+

**Pinehurst (Townhome Villas)**

- Style: Gated townhomes, 1–2 story
- Size: 1,345–2,015 sq ft
- Bedrooms: 2–4
- Garage: Attached 2-car
- Built: 2004–2006
- Features: Covered patios, some golf course/mountain views, HOA-managed maintenance
- Target buyer: Medical professionals, snowbirds, lock-and-leave lifestyle
- Price range today: $450K–$600K

**Silverlake (Gated)**

- Style: Single-story single-family
- Size: 2,025–2,825 sq ft
- Bedrooms: 3–4
- Garages: 2–3 car
- Lot size: ~7,000 sq ft to 1/4 acre
- Built: 2004–2006

**Windermere, Tuscany & The Cottages (Gated)**

- Front-yard maintenance included in HOA
- Professional landscape upkeep exterior
- Flexible floor plans

**Non-Gated Villages (Amberly, Clairbrook, Eastpoint, Greenfield, Parkfield, Somerset)**

- More affordable entry into Silverstone Ranch
- Efficient floor plans, good proximity to schools
- Sizes generally 1,348–2,800 sq ft

### Architectural Style

- **Primary:** Mediterranean Revival — stucco exteriors, clay tile roofs, wrought-iron balconies, arched entryways
- **Secondary:** Contemporary desert — stacked-stone accents, clean lines
- **Interiors:** Soaring ceilings, transom windows, travertine or engineered hardwood floors, open-concept great rooms, oversized kitchen islands, walk-in pantries, spa-like owner suites
- **Smart home features:** Nest thermostats, integrated security systems, EV-ready outlets (increasingly common)
- Common floor plan features: Split bedrooms, open concept, multi-level or single-story options, 3-car garages (select models)

### Overall Size & Price Range

- **Square footage range:** 1,345–4,050 sq ft
- **Current price range:** ~$450K (Pinehurst townhomes) to $1.1M+ (Palms estates)
- **Median sale price (Nov 2025):** $685,000
- **Price per square foot:** $284 average

### Market Trends

- Silverstone Ranch commands a **premium over comparable Northwest communities** due to guard-gated security, mature landscaping, and tight inventory
- **Renovated single-story homes** command 8% premium over two-story due to limited supply
- Primary buyer segments: interstate relocators (particularly CA/WA/NY), medical professionals at Centennial Hills Hospital, Las Vegas locals upgrading to guard-gated
- Multiple-offer situations common on turnkey, well-staged homes
- Buyers often include appraisal gap coverage in competitive offers
- **Rentals:** Min. 6-month leases required by HOA; tenants must register at guard gate

### Historical Price Context

- Homes built at original 2003–2008 sales prices of ~$300K–$500K (new construction era)
- Post-2015 golf course closure created short-term buyer hesitation but prices recovered
- 2020–2023 surge period pushed values significantly higher across all Las Vegas submarkets
- 2024–2025: Stabilization with modest appreciation; tighter inventory supporting price floor

### The Golf Course Factor (Critical Buyer Disclosure)

- The Silverstone Golf Club (27-hole, 270 acres) **closed in 2015**
- Clubhouse (34,000 sq ft) **burned to the ground November 2021** (suspected arson)
- Previous owner went through bankruptcy twice; extensive litigation with HOA
- **Sold at Clark County trustee auction May 8, 2025** for $2.8M (minimum bid) to Khusrow "K" Roohani (entity: Avalon Springs LLC)
- **Deed restriction:** Property must operate as a 27-hole golf course UNLESS 75%+ of Silverstone Ranch homeowners vote to approve alternative use
- As of November 2025: **No redevelopment plans filed** with City of Las Vegas or HOA
- New owner stated intent to consult HOA before proposing any project
- Former fairways now provide open desert views and informal walking/biking access
- **Due diligence checklist for golf-adjacent lots:** Review title report for easements; consult insurance on liability; budget for landscaping/privacy upgrades if desired; review CC&Rs for homeowner voting rights
